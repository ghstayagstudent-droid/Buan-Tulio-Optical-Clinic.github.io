Clinical Eye Examination

Our comprehensive check-up includes more than just a prescription update. We prioritize your ocular health through:

  • Visual Acuity: Standardized clarity testing.
  • Refraction Assessment: Precise measurement for corrective lenses.
  • Ocular Health Screening: Detection of glaucoma and cataracts.
